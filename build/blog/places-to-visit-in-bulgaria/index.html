Places To Visit in Bulgaria

July 8, 2023

•

Travel

  • Bulgaria, located in the southeastern corner of Europe, is a country that beautifully mingles history, tradition, and nature's beauty.
  • Let's embark on a journey through Bulgaria's standout locations that should undoubtedly make your travel list.

Sofia best places to visit

Sofia, Bulgaria's lively capital, typically serves as the initial touchpoint for travelers. This city marries centuries-old landmarks with a pulsating city energy. Highlights include the majestic Alexander Nevsky Cathedral, the tranquil Borisova Gradina Park, and the thriving shopping haven, Vitosha Boulevard.

Alexander Nevsky Cathedral, Sofia

Must see locations in Plovdiv

Plovdiv, with an impressive history spanning over 6000 years of continuous settlement, is a paradise for those who love history and culture. As a previous European Capital of Culture, Plovdiv's Old Town is rich in ancient Roman structures, beautifully refurbished houses showcasing the Bulgarian Revival period, and the vibrant Kapana Art District.

Plovdiv center.

What to see in Varna, the sea capital of Bulgaria?

Varna, Bulgaria's primary seaside city, is celebrated for its sandy beaches, lively nightlife, and a calendar full of summer festivities. The city's Archaeological Museum, which houses the world's most ancient gold treasure, is a must-visit. The sea lantern is one of the most lovely places to go on a walk.

Lighthouse and port in Varna

Veliko Tarnovo: Relive Bulgaria's Medieval Glory

Veliko Tarnovo, once Bulgaria's medieval capital, is famous for its hilltop fortress, Tsarevets, offering panoramic city views. A walk along the quaint Gurko Street will transport you back to an era of Bulgarian royalty and heroic knights.

Bansko: Embrace the Mountains and Traditions

Bansko, situated at the base of the Pirin Mountains, is an irresistible attraction for skiing enthusiasts and nature lovers. Bansko is revered for its ski routes, hiking paths, and traditional "mehana" taverns, where you can savor authentic Bulgarian dishes.

Hotels in Bansko, Pirin mountains in the background.

And so, we've navigated through the enticing destinations Bulgaria has to offer. With its diversity and charm, Bulgaria will undoubtedly leave a lasting imprint on your travel memories. Here's to exploring and discovering!
